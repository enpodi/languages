Swedish word:
(en) klocka

English translation:
clock

Part of speech:
noun

Swedish example:
För trettio år sedan slog blixten ner i det där klocktornet.

English example:
Thirty years ago, lightning struck that clock tower.



(en) historia
history
Part of speech:
noun
Swedish example:
De studerade historia vid universitetet.
English example:
They studied history at the university.


Worth mentioning is that
 Historia and en historia have two different meanings
 Historia is history while en historia means "a, story"
